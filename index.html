From all of us at Eduition

Thank you for your support over the years!

We're proud to have served all of you!

We sought to develop the best possible online teaching platform to offer the best of face-to-face teaching with the versatility of online technology. Given the feedback from everyone, we know we achieved this and so it's with a heavy heart that we have closed Eduition down - for the time being.

Being three recent university graduates just starting full time jobs, we couldn't see a way forward to continue to serve you to the best of our abilities.

We thank everyone for their support and to the many of you who keep asking when we'll be back.
